Pizza

Description

A pizza is a popular dish originating from Italy, now beloved worldwide. It consists of a flat, round base made from yeast-leavened dough, typically topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various ingredients, then baked in an oven. Pizzas can range from simple classics like Margherita, which features fresh basil, mozzarella, and tomatoes, to more elaborate variations with a variety of meats, vegetables, and gourmet toppings.

The crust can vary in style, from thin and crispy to thick and fluffy, like the deep-dish pizzas of Chicago. The sauce is usually a savory tomato-based mixture, sometimes replaced with alternatives like pesto or white garlic sauce. Cheese, especially mozzarella, is a key component, providing a gooey and satisfying texture.

Ingredients

  • Dough:
    • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
    • 1 1/2 cups warm water
    • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Sauce:
    • 1 cup tomato sauce
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Toppings:
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Sliced pepperoni, vegetables, or other favorite toppings

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • Dissolve yeast in warm water and let sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
    • In a large bowl, mix flour, salt, and sugar. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for 8-10 minutes until smooth.
    • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let rise for 1-2 hours.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    • Mix tomato sauce, oregano, basil, and garlic in a bowl. Set aside.
  3. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
    • Roll out the dough on a floured surface to fit your pizza pan or baking sheet.
    • Spread the sauce evenly over the dough.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top, followed by your favorite toppings.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  5. Slice and serve hot!
